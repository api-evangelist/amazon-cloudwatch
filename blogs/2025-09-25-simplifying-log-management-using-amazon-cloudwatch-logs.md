Managing logs across multiple AWS accounts and regions has always been a complex challenge for organizations. As AWS infrastructure grows to include separate accounts for production, development, and staging environments, along with regions, the complexity of log management increases exponentially. During critical incidents, especially during off-hours, teams spend valuable time, searching through multiple accounts, correlating events across different regions, managing complex log aggregation systems, and maintaining cross-account access permissions.
